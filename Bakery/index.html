About Iyengar Bakery

Iyengar's Bakery has been serving delicious traditional cakes and sweets in Bengaluru for decades. Our mission is to preserve the taste of authentic recipes while bringing innovative flavors to our customers.

The bakery is managed by Arun Kumar, a passionate baker who ensures quality in every bite. From birthdays to weddings, our bakery caters to all occasions with love and care.
