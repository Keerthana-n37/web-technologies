My Projects

Data Structure Visualization

Data structure visualization is the graphical representation of how data is organized, stored, and manipulated within different data structures such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs. It helps learners and developers understand complex operations like insertion, deletion, searching, and traversal by displaying them in an interactive and visual format rather than just code. By using diagrams, animations, and color-coded elements, visualization makes it easier to see how data moves and changes step by step. This approach improves problem-solving skills, enhances learning, and makes abstract concepts more concrete and easier to grasp, especially for beginners in computer science

Panchayat Union Automation System

A Panchayat Union Automation System is a digital solution designed to streamline and manage the administrative functions of rural local bodies efficiently. It helps automate tasks such as record maintenance, tax collection, public service delivery, complaint management, and development project tracking. By replacing manual paperwork with computerized processes, the system improves accuracy, transparency, and speed in governance. It also enables easy access to citizen information, generates reports, and supports better decision-making for officials. Overall, the automation system enhances accountability, reduces delays, and ensures effective delivery of services to rural communities.
